# Zara_Sales
An interactive sales performance dashboard built in Power BI analyzing ₹37.69M in revenue across product categories, sections, promotions, and seasonal trends.
📌 Project Overview
This dashboard provides a complete overview of Zara's sales performance, enabling business stakeholders to monitor revenue, track top products, and evaluate the impact of promotions and seasonality — all without analyst support.

🔍 Visuals Included

Revenue by Category — Horizontal bar chart ranking jackets, sweaters, shoes, t-shirts, and jeans by total revenue
Revenue by Section — Donut chart showing 91.31% ($34.41M) vs 8.69% ($3.28M) section split
Sales Volume by Promotion — Comparison of promoted vs non-promoted product sales (0.24M vs 0.22M)
Revenue by Seasonal — Side-by-side column chart: Seasonal vs Non-Seasonal revenue (~$19M each)
Top 10 Products by Name — Bar chart ranking top revenue-generating products, led by Contrasting Patches Bomber Jacket ($0.89M)


🎛️ Filters / Slicers

Section

💡 Key Insights

Jackets dominate revenue across all categories by a significant margin
91% of revenue comes from a single section — a potential risk worth flagging to leadership
Promoted products marginally outsell non-promoted ones (0.24M vs 0.22M), suggesting promotions have modest but measurable impact
Seasonal and non-seasonal products generate nearly equal revenue (~$19M each), indicating year-round demand stability


🛠️ Tools Used

Microsoft Power BI Desktop
DAX — for KPI measures (Total Revenue, Avg Price, Promotion %)
Data Modeling — relationships between product, section, and sales tables
Power Query — data cleaning and transformation
